Crystal Collector

Target Number:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of crystals to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The amount of crystals that each character can grab is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each character and a new random number will be generated.
